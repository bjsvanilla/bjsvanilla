About Ben

Ben is originally from Wagga Wagga. His father was a painter and Ben completed his Painting and Decorating Apprenticeship while working in the family business. Since then, Ben moved to Sydney where he started his painting business B. J. Shields Painting and Decorating. The main focus of the business was on residential work. After 10 years of running the business in Sydney, Ben moved to the Sunshine Coast for the lifestyle. He continues to run the same high quality painting service on the Sunshine Coast and is looking forward to building clientele and creating the same great relationships he had with his clients in Sydney.
